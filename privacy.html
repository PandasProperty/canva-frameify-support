Privacy Policy

Overview

Frameify is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your data — in short, we don't collect any.

Data Collection

Frameify does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, or any other data collection mechanisms.

Image Processing

All image processing happens entirely within your browser. When you upload a PNG or select an image from your Canva canvas, the App processes it locally using JavaScript to trace the image outline. Your images are never sent to any external server, and they are not stored anywhere beyond your current browser session.

No Account Required

Frameify does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information. The App runs within your existing Canva session.

No Third-Party Services

Frameify does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms. The App operates entirely within the Canva environment using the Canva Apps SDK.

Data Storage

Frameify does not store any data locally or remotely. No cookies, local storage, or session storage is used. Each time you open the App, it starts fresh with no memory of previous sessions.

Children's Privacy

Frameify does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since the App collects no data at all, this applies universally to all users.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update this page. Since the App collects no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.

Contact
